About Us

Morisco is an independent game studio dedicated to crafting original video games. We believe that games are more than entertainment, they are a way to tell stories, spark imagination, and connect players with worlds full of wonder. Our inspiration comes from rich cultural tales and timeless legends that have been passed down through generations. By reimagining these stories in an interactive form, we aim to preserve their magic while offering fresh and engaging gameplay. Passionate about history, fantasy, and video games, we strive to blend these themes into unique experiences that challenge the mind and inspire the heart. Our first game is designed to combine strategy, chance, and narrative depth, ensuring that players are not only entertained but also immersed in meaningful adventures. At Morisco, our mission is to create games that stand out for their originality, their storytelling, and their ability to transport players to new horizons where every choice shapes the journey, and every adventure leaves a lasting impression.

Our Games

Sinbad Stories: A Card Game

Set sail on a rich narrative journey inspired by the legendary voyages of Sinbad the Sailor. Sinbad Stories is a solo, story-driven card game that blends thoughtful resource management with immersive storytelling. With every card you play, you steer the course of a grand adventure full of wonder, mystery, and peril.

In this beautifully illustrated solo experience, you travel across the seas from Baghdad to remote islands, ancient ruins, and mythical lands. Along the way, you'll encounter strange creatures, mysterious events, and story moments that unfold based on the cards you play.
